The What's, When's,
and How's

Everything you need to know before you take the next step.

What's included when I lease a suite?
You get a private, move-in ready space with 24/7 access, building security, surveillance, and parking. The suite is yours to set up, decorate, and brand exactly the way you want.
What equipment is provided?
Your suite is a blank canvas — designed so your tools, your style, and your personality are what define the space. Bring your kit, set your scene, and make it entirely yours.
When can I access my suite?
Anytime. There are no time restrictions — your suite runs 24/7. You set your own schedule and work the hours that work for you and your clients.
When do payments happen?
Payments are made weekly from the start of your lease, keeping your cash flow simple and predictable.
How does leasing work?
Leases start at a minimum of 1 year. We offer five suite options — Value, Standard, Premier, Double, and Share — so you only pay for what fits your practice.
How do I grow my business from here?
At signing, you get listed on the MySalonSuite website as a resident professional plus a digital advertising kit to grow your reach and attract new clients from day one — and you keep 100% of everything you earn.
